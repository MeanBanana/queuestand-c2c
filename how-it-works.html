How it Works

What is Queue Stand

Queue Stander is a secure online marketplace that connects people who don't have time to wait in long queues with trusted standers who can wait on their behalf.

Whether it's at Department of Home Affairs, South African Social Security Agency (SASSA), banks, clinics, or licensing offices — Queue Stander helps you save time while creating income opportunities for others.

For Job Posters

Post a Job

Create an account and post a job with:

  • Location (e.g FNB The Glen)
  • Date and Time
  • Estimated waiting time
  • Payment amount
  • Special instructions if needed

Choose A Verified Stander

Each stander has verified ID, ratings, reviews, and completed job history. Select the stander you trust the most.

Secure Payment

Your payment is held securely until the job is completed, protecting both you and the stander.

Real-Time Updates

Receive updates when the stander arrives, your position changes, and when it's almost your turn.

Confirm and Rate

Once completed, confirm the job, and the stander receives payment. Leave a rating and review.

For Queue Standers

Sign Up and Verify

Create a profile and complete ID verification to build trust and increase your chances of getting jobs.

Browse Available Jobs

See nearby jobs based on your location, availability, and preferred working hours. Accept jobs that suit your schedule.

Stand in the Queue

Arrive at the location, check in through the platform, provide updates to the job poster, and follow instructions.

Build Your Reputation

The more jobs you complete successfully, the higher your rating and the more you can potentially earn.

How We Keep You Safe

We understand that trust is important. That's why Queue Stander includes:

  • ID verification
  • Secure Escrow Payments
  • Dispute Resolution Support
  • Location-Based Matching

Our goal is to reduce fraud, prevent disputes, and create a safe environment for both sides.
